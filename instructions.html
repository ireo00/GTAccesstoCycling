Instructions

The GTAccess to Cycling Map simultaneously provides users with a comprehensive view of cycling resources available in the GTA, a real time update of traffic and weather conditions, and a planning tool to plan their upcoming cycling trips.

To get a comprehensive overview of cycling resources and current conditions...

Use the toggle menu by clicking on the check boxes for each of the bicycle shops, parking, and shares, to control which of these facilities are visible on the map.

Use the dropdown menu for GTA streets to select whether traffic or bikeways (1 type or all types) are visible on the map. Use the weather dropdown menu to select which weather conditions are viewed on the map.

To plan your next biking trip...

Click the "Plan Your Trip!" menu. It will prompt you to select any location on the map by clicking on it - the search function can also be used to find a location before clicking on it.

Once the location is selected, click "GO". A slider will appear which allows you to select the size of the buffer region surrounding the point. Toggle the slider to select desired buffer size.

For every selected buffer, a list of all available amenities (bike shops, bike parkings, bike shares) within the buffer radius, as well as a summary of weather conditions at the clicked point, will be provided. To select a new point, click 'Close' and repeat these steps.
