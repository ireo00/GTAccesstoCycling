Purpose

Objectives

As cities continue to grow and the number of cars on the roads increases, urban mobility becomes an increasingly important issue. Traffic congestion, air pollution, and increased greenhouse gas emissions are some challenges posed by this growth in traffic. Active transportation, such as cycling, can be an effective solution to these challenges while also promoting physical activity and healthy living. Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has made cycling more popular, as many people see it as a safer and more socially distanced mode of transportation.

Our project aims to inspire more people to start cycling in the city by developing a web application that caters to a diverse audience. We hope to provide a wide range of cycling-related information and resources in one location, from introductory safety information to advanced planning tools for current and potential cyclists. The focus of our app is the Great Toronto Area (GTA), which is divided into five regions: Toronto, Durham, Halton, Peel, and York regions.
